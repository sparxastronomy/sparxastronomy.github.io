Research Projects

Summer project in 2021

I worked under the guidance of Dr. Rajesh Kumble Nayak, where I learned about gravitational waves and how to detect them. I learned about parameter estimations for gravitational waves and used the python package 'PyCBC' to estimate the parameters. I also learned about the particle swarm optimization approach to parameter estimations.

Radio Astronomy Winter School - 2020

I attended the radio astronomy winter school -2020, Jointly organized by IUCAA-Pune and NCRA. It was a 10 day school where we learnt about the basics of Radio astronomy and in the end we presented analysis of a given data. The reports and the presentation slides can be found here.


Research in Plasma Physics

I attended plasma physics workshop organized by Dr. Swarniv Chandra, where we learnt about basic plasma physics. Then I worked on Quantum Plasma, where I obtained the Dispersion Relation for Electron Acoustic Wave in two electrons populated, dense Fermi plasma, and further worked to get the KdV equation for the same. I studied the dependence of solitary structures on various plasma parameters.



Imaging Projects

Chandra Imaging

This is the album containing my work on Chandra data. This contains images made out of only Chandra data, as well, images made from data combining other telescopes well. Don't forget to check out N132D SNR or other cool targets.
Working on Chandra data is really fun and enlightening. It reveals so much of the unexplored universe, which is violent. Moreover Processes that generate X-ray emissions are different from those of optical, and gives us valuable information about the surroundings. Like accretion jets from a SMBH, or structures of SNRs.

Most of the materials that is there on earth was once at the heart of a dying star, basically we are made of cosmic dust. Doesn't this inspires you that we withhold some cosmic mysteries?


Hubble Imaging

This album contains my works using data of Hubble space telescope, and images in which hubble data is used along with other survey. Hubble changed the way we understand the universe today. It studied a lot of objects in the universe and provided us with some solid proof of General Relativity like the Einstein's Ring. Combing Hubble's data with other surveys shows some really cool stuffs. Do check it out.
